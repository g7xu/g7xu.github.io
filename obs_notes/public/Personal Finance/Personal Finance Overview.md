The whole cluster is built around one idea: **money flows down a priority list**, and you only move to the next step once the current one is satisfied. The centerpiece is the flowchart hub; the rest are one page per step.

## Start here

- [[Personal Finance Order of Operations]] — the flowchart hub: the ordered priority list for every dollar of income, with the "why this order" rationale (guaranteed risk-free returns before uncertain market returns)

## The ordered steps

- [[Budgeting]] — **Step 0**: know where your money goes, cut expenses, set realistic goals
- [[Emergency Fund]] — **Step 1**: a 3–6 month liquid reserve (or a $1,000 starter while paying off debt)
- [[401(k) Employer Match]] — **Step 2**: capture the free employer match before paying off debt
- [[High-Interest Debt Payoff]] — **Step 3**: avalanche vs. snowball for debts above ~4%
- [[IRA & Roth vs Traditional]] — **Steps 4–5**: fund an IRA, then round out retirement to 15–20% (incl. backdoor / mega-backdoor Roth)
- [[Saving for Other Goals]] — **Step 6**: match the account to the goal's time horizon
- [[HSA & 529 Accounts]] — **Step 6 specialized accounts**: HSA for medical, 529 for education
